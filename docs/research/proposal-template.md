# Website Project Proposal Template

## Organisation Overview
- Name: Sweet Heaven's Bakery
- Brief history: Sweet Heaven's Bakery began as a small family owned business with a simple dream
        to share the joy of freshly baked goods with its local community. Founded in the year 2026.
        the bakery started with just a handful of recipes passed down through generations. Such as
        rich chocolate cakes, buttery croissants, and delicate pastries.

        The bakery serves only a few loyal customers, but word quickly spread about its warm atmosphere
        and the care put into every product.
        By combining traditional baking methods with creative flavors,
        Sweet Heaven's Bakery became known not just for its pastries but for its way of expressing love and creativity.

- Mission: At Sweet Heaven's Bakery, our mission is to provide delicious baked goods that are lovingly crafted with care,
        creativity, and the finest ingredients.
        We believe that baking is more than just a skill — it is an art form and a heartfelt way to bring joy to others.
        Every cake, pastry, and dessert we create is designed to delight our customers and make their special moments
        even sweeter.
        Alongside our commitment to quality, we strive to deliver excellent customer service, ensuring that every person
        who walks through our doors feels valued, welcomed,
        and part of our community. By combining passion, tradition, and innovation, we aim to build lasting
        relationships and become a trusted bakery brand both locally and online.

- Vision: At Sweet Heaven's Bakery, our vision is to become a beloved destination where every customer feels at home and
        every bite tells a story of passion and care.
        We aspire to be recognized not only for our aesthetics and signature baked goods,
        but also for creating memorable experiences that bring people together. By blending tradition with innovation,
        we aim to expand our reach beyond our local community, offering our creations to a wider audience while staying
        true to our values of quality, creativity,
        and heartfelt service. Ultimately, our vision is to inspire joy and connection through the art of baking.

- Target audience:
 •	Local residents
•	Event planners
•	Customers seeking fresh, homemade baked goods


## Website Goals and Objectives
- Goal 1: Deliver Quality Baked Goods
To consistently provide high quality cakepastries, and desserts made with the finest ingredients and traditional methods, ensuring every product delights customers and reflects the bakery's passion for excellence.

- Goal 2: Strengthen Customer Relationships
To build lasting connections with the community by offering excellent customer service, creating a welcoming atmosphere and making it easy for customers to engage with the bakery both in store and online.

- Goal 3:Expand Brand Presence
To grow beyond the local market by promoting the bakery's identity online, showcasing its products through the website, and reaching new audiences while staying true to its values of creativity, tradition, and love for baking.
